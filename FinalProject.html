About:

The PARK IT app is for going to places such as tourist attractions or or out to a normally crowded location where parking may be limited. Users can type in their destination and be directed to a close parking spot which is determined by camera's and satalites to show where the spot is. The GPS on the app will give you specific directions on how to get to that spot quickly. If someone takesthe spot, the user will be notified and be given a new spot in less than five minutes.
