Select Your Destination

Selecting your destination is the first step in finding a great parking spot for you before you arrive. You will have the closest spot possible to your location.

Directional Navigation for Parking

The app provided multiple options for parking spaces as well as a picture included. The GPS provided through the app will give you guided directions to your selected parking spot.

Personalized Parking Spots

You will never have to worry about making it to a parking spot with other app users. Each spot is personalized once selected.

Interview for Creative Thinking

Before I began designing the app, I interviewed a friend who travels during the summer with her family every year. I asked the following questions:

Q: What challenges do you and your family find every summer when traveling?
A: The challenges we faced the most are finding tourist attractions that arent packed and finding a parking space.

Q: How long does it take for you to find a spot and get in the door?
A: It usually takes a while depending on how popular the attraction is. Two Summers ago we went to Paris and purchased our own rental car so we could travel freely, but it took us nearly two hours to find any good spots.

Results


The results of the interview have led me to think of an app that has something to do with parking and making it a lot easier for those who travel to lots of packed tourist attractions.

Brainstorming

While thinking about how the app could help solve problems, I thought of three main focuses which are User Experience overall, Location, and Convience. I asked myself three "How might we...?" questions to help find solutions to each focus:

1. How might the app I create find convient parking spaces for the users?
2. How might I find new spaces once one is taken?
3. How might the app be easy to use and personalized for each user?

When Do I Use It?


The best time to use this app is for going to places such as tourist attractions or or out to a normally crowded location where parking may be limited. The user an type in their destination and be directed to a close parking spot which is determined by camera's and satalites to show where the spot is. The GPS on the app will give you specific directions on how to get to that spot quickly. If someone takesthe spot, the user will be notified and be given a new spot in less than five minutes.
